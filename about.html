About Us

We are a non-government organization that provides quality care and support to orphaned and vulnerable children. SOS CHILDREN'S VILLAGES was founded by Herman Gmeiner with vison of providing orphaned and abandoned children with a loving family, home, and community. Herman Gmeiner founded this organisation during World War II. This organisation was established in 1984 in South Africa, supporting children and young people without parental care.
