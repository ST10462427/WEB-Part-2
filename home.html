Welcome to SOS Children's Village in South Africa

Our Goal is to help children that are orphanes and abandoned have a place to call home: People don't know that for children their own culture or belief comes from a place where their needs are met such as food, helthcare and a shelter. SOS Children Village in South Africa offers children a place that takes them away from being alone, to a place where they can be children again and play and do all the things that children with parents have the privilige to.
